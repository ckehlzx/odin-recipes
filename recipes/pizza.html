Pepperoni Pizza

An Italian treat

Pizza is one of those things that needs no explanation.. so without furthur ado, let's get right into the ingredients and recipe!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Take out the dough.
  2. Cover it in cheese.
  3. Put on some pepperonis.
  4. Bake it in the oven.
