California Roll

A light dish from Japan combined with American taste

Ingredients

  • Nori
  • Sushi Rice
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Crab meat

Instructions

  1. Lay down the nori.
  2. Put a small amount of rice and spread it over the nori.
  3. Place the onion, crab and avocado on top of the rice.
  4. Slowly roll the nori so that it does not break.
  5. Slice and serve.
